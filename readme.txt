Create channels of Android, iPhone or iPad apps and games, write reviews about them and let your visitors see them on your website in a beautiful widget. Supports shortcodes for embedding in blog posts.

== Description ==

Express yourself with the Playboard widget by collecting your favorite apps in a channel and sharing them with your friends and the whole Playboard community. Playboard users will see your channels in the app or on the website (http://playboard.me), subscribe to them and can even go to your website for a full review. By using the Playboard Widget you can easily showcase your channels in a beautiful way on your website.

The widget can be customized to fit any size and is installed as a Main Sidebar widget. See an example on the Playboard website - http://playboard.me/widgets/

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `playboard_channel_box.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Add the widget to your Main Sidebar by clicking on Appearance->Widgets and drag-and-dropping it on the Main Sidebar. The widget's configuration form appears and you can start using it on your blog.
